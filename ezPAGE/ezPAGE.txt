This is ezPAGE the Software for controlling ezPOC8. It has the ability to connect to the DAPNET. 
Use ezPAGE -h for help/ further information.
Credentials for your DAPNET transmitter account are written in the DAPNET.h auth-String (https://hampager.de/dokuwiki/doku.php?id=dapnetprotocol).
Just compile ist with the g++ command.
